ABOUT

Hello! I'm a high school student with a passion for all things paper and handmade. I've been making books since I was seven years old, and throughout the years I've learned many new techniques and improved my bookmaking methods. Every book and binder at Candlelit Binding is handcrafted with the highest quality eco-friendly materials, like 100% recycled Davey Board for the covers and 50% recycled paper for the text blocks. Whether you're looking for a journal, travel diary, sketchbook, or 3-ring binder, Candlelit Binding has it all. To place an order, just email or text me the books and/or binders you'd like. Thanks for supporting my craft and helping me share the art of bookmaking!!!
